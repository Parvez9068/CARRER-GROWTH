Help Center

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the instructions to register with your email address and password.

How can I post a job?

To post a job, you need to log in to your recruiter account and navigate to the "Post Jobs" section. Fill in the job details and submit the form to publish the job listing.

How do I apply for a job?

To apply for a job, browse through the job listings on the homepage, and click on the job title to view the job details. Then, click on the "Apply Now" button and follow the instructions to submit your application.
